A Lifelong Passion for Writing

I've always had a passion for writing and for communication of ideas. My time at Rice has given me a myriad of opportunities to hone my written communication abilities and share my talents with my peers. Since August 2023, I've acted as a Technical Writing Mentor for ENGI 120, Rice's introductory Engineering Design course. This roel has involved giving students direct feedback on the clarity and conciseness of technical memos about their design projects, through the medium of regular meetings and written comments and changes. My effectiveness as a mentor is regularly evaluated through CATME surveys, where students have reported increases as high as 20 percent in their grades due to the writing advice provided to them through these meetings

In addition to my role as an advisor, I also take on an intermediate role as a Staff Writer for my school's newspaper, the Rice Thresher.
